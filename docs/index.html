About Us
Vortex is a group of students from Delhi Public School, Rohini who share a passion for Mathematics and want to share this with others through various events ranging from problem solving to quizzing. The symposium is an opportunity for the students who wish to expand their horizons of learning and provide a platform for young minds to display their talent and skill in mathematics. It will stimulate a competitive environment and compel participants to push their boundaries in the field of mathematics.
STAY IN THE LOOP
Follow us on our socials for the latest updates, mind-bending math challenges, event highlights, and behind-the-scenes moments from the vibrant Vortex community at DPS Rohini. Whether you're a puzzle enthusiast, a quiz whiz, or just someone who loves exploring the beauty of numbers, our socials are your gateway to the action. Be part of a growing network of young minds driven by curiosity, creativity, and a passion for mathematics. Let’s learn, compete, and celebrate the joy of problem-solving — together.
